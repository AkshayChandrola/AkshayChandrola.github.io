▶ Profile

I'm a Material Flow Simulation & Intralogistics Enthusiast with proven expertise in Siemens Tecnomatix Plant Simulation and Python-based discrete event simulation. With a Master's degree in Systems Engineering for Manufacturing from Otto-von-Guericke Universität Magdeburg, I have built a strong foundation in designing and optimizing automated material handling and warehouse systems for efficient, cost-effective production flows.

I'm experienced in supplier communication, cross-functional teamwork, and driving continuous improvement in logistics and supply chain environments.

I'm open-minded, adaptable, and eager to learn new tools and methods to add value to diverse engineering projects. Whether it's building a digital twin, analyzing bottlenecks, or comparing simulation frameworks, I approach every challenge with data-driven rigor and an eye for practical impact.
